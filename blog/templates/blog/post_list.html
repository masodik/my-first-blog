The articularis temporomandibularis

The title seems to be a very difficult and incomprehensible one, but i will translate it for you. The articularis means joint in latin.
So we are discussing a joint right now.I have already studied the bones of the skull, among them I wrote about the temporal bone and the mandibula. This joint is found where these bones meet. Hence its name.
